# Merchant Account

The **Merchant Account** settings page displays the payment provider connected to the platform and allows users to copy merchant identification details for support or integration purposes.
---
## Connected Merchant Account

This section shows the currently connected payment provider and account identifier.

| **Field** | **Description** |
| --- | --- |
| **Payment provider** | The payment processor connected to the account (e.g., **Stripe**). |
| **Merchant account status** | Indicates that a merchant account is successfully connected. |
| **Stripe Account ID** | The unique identifier of the connected Stripe account (e.g., `acct_1RTfmX4WJwnEBta1`). |
---
## Actions

| **Action** | **Description** |
| --- | --- |
| **Click Stripe Account ID** | Copies the Merchant Account ID to the clipboard for easy sharing or support use. |
---
## Notes

- The merchant account ID is commonly required for troubleshooting, support requests, or advanced integrations.
- Only one merchant account can be connected at a time.
- Changes to the merchant account typically require reauthorization with the payment provider.
